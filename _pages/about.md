## Research Interest ##
I am interested in **machine learning driven by both data and domain knowledge**, especially provably effective algorithms. I focus on the following two concrete areas.
### Machine learning strengthened by domain knowledge. ###
Despite its success in computer vision, natual language processing, robotics, etc, machine learning has the potential in broader areas of engineering, science and social goods. In these areas, many problems are traditionally solved by models from domain knowledge which are developed by domain experts. Nevertheless, in many cases, models from domain knowledge cannot perfectly represent the real world, so the methods purely driven by domain knowledge may have low performance in practice. Therefore, it is necessary to use methods driven by both data and domain knowledge.
