## Research Interest ##
I am interested in the broad area of machine learning, especially provably effective algorithms. I focus on the following two concrete areas.
### Learning driven by both data and domain knowledge. ###
Machine learning has the potential to be applied in broader domains of engineering, science and social goods. Typically, domain knowledge like models and equations exist in these areas. We aim to develop several genral learning frameworks driven by domain-specific knowledge and data.
### Online decision making assisted by knowledge. ###
Online decision making including bandit learning, reinforcement learning, Bayesian optimization has been used in many fields. We aim to develop online algorithms based on context or domain knowledge.
